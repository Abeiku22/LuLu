Drink to me only with thine eyes, And I will pledge with mine

But thou thereon didst only breathe,And sentst it back to me

Since when it grows and smells, I swear, Not of itself, but thee

Were all a little weird. And life is a little weird. And when we

find someone whose weirdness is compatible with ours, we join up with them

and fall into mutually satisfying weirdness—and call it love—true love.

Sometimes, your nearness takes my breath away, and all the things

I want to say can find no voice. Then, in silence, I can only hope my eyes will speak my heart.
